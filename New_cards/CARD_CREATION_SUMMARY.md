### ğŸ”§ **How It Works**

#### **Step 1: Select a Card**
- Left sidebar shows all available card images from your folder
- Click on any card to select it
- Selected card appears in the center display area

#### **Step 2: Fill Required Fields**
- Right panel contains common fields:
  - **Name** - Full name
  - **ID Number** - Employee/ID number
  - **Title/Position** - Job title
  - **Department** - Department name
  - **Email** - Email address
  - **Phone** - Phone number
  - **Address** - Physical address
  - **Company** - Company name

#### **Step 3: Add Fields to Card**
- Type information in any field
- Click "Add to Card" button
- Text appears on the card image
- Drag text to position it exactly where needed

#### **Step 4: Save or Download**
- **Save Card**: Saves to server
- **Download**: Downloads to your computer
- **Reset**: Returns card to original state
